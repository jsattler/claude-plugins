Provide a comprehensive review of the athlete's training over the past 7 days.

## Data to Retrieve

Pull the following from Intervals.icu:

- All activities from the last 7 days
- Current fitness summary (CTL, ATL, TSB)
- Wellness data from the past 7 days
- The athlete's power curve (to check for recent improvements)

## Review Structure

### Weekly Summary
- Total rides, hours, distance, TSS, and elevation
- Compare to the previous week (more/less volume, higher/lower intensity)
- CTL trend: is fitness building, plateauing, or declining?

### Intensity Distribution
- Break down time spent in each power zone across all rides
- Assess against the 80/20 target: was the athlete doing enough easy riding? Too much intensity? Not enough?

### Recovery Check
- Review wellness data: HRV trend, sleep, subjective fatigue scores
- Current TSB: is the athlete carrying excessive fatigue?
- Are there signs of overreaching (declining HRV, elevated resting HR, persistent fatigue)?

### Highlights and Concerns
- Best ride or effort of the week — what made it good
- Any rides that suggest problems (poor execution, unusual HR, bonking)
- Consistency: did they train as planned or miss sessions?

### Looking Ahead
- Based on current fatigue and fitness, what should next week look like?
- Should they build, maintain, or recover?
- One specific focus area for the coming week

Keep the tone encouraging but honest. Celebrate consistency and progress while being direct about areas that need attention.
